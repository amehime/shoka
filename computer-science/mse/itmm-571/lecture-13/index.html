Lecture 13. Stakeholder Management

# Identify Stakeholders 确定利益相关者

  • Identifying the people, groups, organizations
    识别人员，团体，组织
  • Documenting stakeholder information
    记录利益相关者信息
  • Defining how the stakeholders could affect the project
    定义利益相关者如何影响项目

# Who are stakeholders? 谁是利益相关者？

  • Persons and organizations
    个人和组织
  • Involved in the project
    参与项目
  • Affected positively or negatively by project
    受项目正面或负面影响
  • Some can exert influence over the project
    有些可以对项目产生影响

# Identifying Project Stakeholders 确定项目利益相关者

  • People and groups affected by the project
    受项目影响的人群
  • Stakeholder exert influence over the project
    利益相关者对项目产生影响
  • Identify early in the project
    尽早确定项目
  • Stakeholder management strategy
    利益相关者管理策略
  • Classify stakeholders according to:
    根据以下内容对利益相关者进行分类：
    • Interest 兴趣
    • Influence 影响
    • Involvement 参与度

# Stakeholder Analysis 利益相关者分析

  • Identify all potential stakeholders and info
    确定所有潜在的利益相关者和信息
  • Key stakeholders are
    主要利益相关者是
    • Decision-making role
      决策角色
    • Management role
      管理角色
    • Primary customer
      主要客户
  • Interview stakeholders to identify stakeholders
    采访利益相关者以识别利益相关者
  • Power/Interest Grid
    力量 / 利益 坐标

# Stakeholder Register 利益相关者登记册

  • Identification information
    识别信息
  • Assessment information
    评估信息
  • Stakeholder classification
    利益相关者分类

# Plan Stakeholder Management 计划利益相关者管理

  • Developing management strategies for stakeholder engagement
    制定利益相关者参与的管理策略
  • Analysis of stakeholder needs
    利益相关者需求分析
  • Creates a clear plan for managing the stakeholders
    为管理利益相关者制定清晰的计划
  • Expert judgment for stakeholder management planning
    利益相关者管理计划的专家判断
    • Senior management
      高级管理人员
    • Project team members
      项目团队成员
    • Organizational resources
      组织资源
    • Identified key stakeholders
      确定主要利益相关者
    • Project managers
      项目经理
    • Subject matter experts
      主题专家
    • Regulatory bodies and nongovernmental agencies
      监管机构和非政府机构

# Stakeholder Engagement Levels 利益相关者参与度

  • Unaware
    不知道
  • Resistant
    抵抗的
  • Neutral
    中性的
  • Supportive
    支持性
  • Leading
    领导

# Reviewing the stakeholder management plan 审查利益相关者管理计划

  • Desired and current engagement levels
    期望的和当前的参与度
  • Scope and impact of change to stakeholders
    变更的范围和对利益相关者的影响
  • Identified interrelationships and potential overlap
    确定的相互关系和潜在的重叠
  • Stakeholder communication requirements
    利益相关者的沟通要求
  • Information to be distributed
    信息发布
  • Reason for the distribution of that information
    分发该信息的原因
  • Time frame and frequency for the distribution of required information
    分发所需信息的时间范围和频率

# Manage Stakeholder Engagement 管理利益相关者的参与

  • Engaging stakeholders as needed in the project
    在项目中根据需要吸引利益相关者
  • Obtain, confirm, maintain stakeholder commitment to project
    获得，确认，维持利益相关者对项目的承诺
  • Manage stakeholder expectations
    管理利益相关者的期望
  • Address potential concerns
    解决潜在的问题
  • Clarifying and resolving issues
    澄清和解决问题

# Methods to engage stakeholders 吸引利益相关者的方法

  • Communication methods
    沟通方式
  • Interpersonal skills
    人际交往能力
    • Building trust
      建立信任
    • Resolving conflict
      解决冲突
    • Active listening
      积极倾听
    • Overcoming resistance to change
      克服变化的阻力
  • Management skills
    管理技巧
    • Facilitate consensus
      促进共识
    • Influence people
      影响人们
    • Negotiate agreements
      谈判协议
    • “Modify organizational behavior to accept the project outcomes”
      “修改组织行为以接受项目成果”

# Control Stakeholder Engagement 控制利益相关者参与

  • Monitoring overall stakeholder relationships
    监控整体利益相关者关系
  • Adjusting stakeholder management strategies
    调整利益相关者管理策略
  • Updating stakeholder management plan as needed
    根据需要更新利益相关者管理计划
  • Approach evolves as project continues
    随着项目的进行，方法不断发展

# Actively engaging stakeholders 积极促进利益相关者

  • Meeting with stakeholders
    与利益相关者会面
  • Being honest and direct with project news
    对项目新闻诚实和直接
项目管理
