i.e.,

Z=Xˉ−μσ/n∼N(0,1)Z = \frac{\bar{X}-\mu}{\sigma/\sqrt{n}} \sim \mathcal{N}(0,1)Z=σ/n​Xˉ−μ​∼N(0,1)

Suppose zα/2z_{\alpha/2}zα/2​ denote the value such that

As confidence is 95%, we know α=1−95%=0.05\alpha = 1-95\% = 0.05α=1−95%=0.05, that is α/2=0.025\alpha/2 = 0.025α/2=0.025.
由于置信度为 95%，则 α=1−95%=0.05\alpha = 1-95\% = 0.05α=1−95%=0.05，即 α/2=0.025\alpha/2 = 0.025α/2=0.025。
