Figure 2.1. from ISLR: Y = Sales plottedagainst TV , Radio and Newspaper advertising budgets.
来自 ISLR：Y = Sales ，根据 TV 、 Radio 和 Newspper 三种途径的广告预算绘制。

Our goal is to develop an accurate model (fff) that can be used to predict sales on the basis of the three media budgets:
我们的目标是开发一个准确的模型 (fff)，可以用来根据三种媒体预算预测 sales 销售额：

Sales≈f(TV,Radio,Newspaper).Sales \approx f(TV, Radio, Newspaper).Sales≈f(TV,Radio,Newspaper).

  • Sales = a reponse, target, or outcome.
    Sales 是响应、目标、或结果。

    • The variable we want to predicit.
      是我们想要预测的变量。
    • Denoted by YYY.
      用YYY 表示。

  • TV is one of the features, or inputs.
    TV 是特征之一，或输入。

    • Denoted by X1X_1X1​.
      用X1X_1X1​ 表示。

  • Similarly for Radio and Newspaper .
    另外两者也类似

  • We can put all the predictors into a single input vector
    我们可以将所有的预测变量放入一个输入向量中

    X=(X1,X2,X3)X = (X_1,X_2,X_3)X=(X1​,X2​,X3​)

  • Now we can write our model as
    现在我们可以将我们的模型写成

    Y=f(X)+ϵY=f(X) +\epsilonY=f(X)+ϵ

    , where ϵ\epsilonϵ captures measurement errors and other discrepancies between the response YYY and the model fff.
    其中 ϵ\epsilonϵ 捕获测量误差，以及响应变量YYY 和模型fff 之间的其他差异。

# Regression function 回归函数

Formally, the regression function is given by E(Y∣X=x)E(Y | X = x)E(Y∣X=x). This is the expected value of YYY at X=xX = xX=x.
形式上，回归函数由 E(Y∣X=x)E(Y | X = x)E(Y∣X=x) 给出。这是YYY 在 X=XX = XX=X 时的期望值。
The ideal or optimal predictor of YYY based on XXX is thus
基于XXX，YYY 的理想或最佳预测变量是这样的

f(x)=E(Y∣X=x)f(x) = E(Y | X = x)f(x)=E(Y∣X=x)

A good value is $$ f(4) = E(Y | X = 4) $$

# Simple linear regression using a single predictor XXX 使用单一预测值的简单线性回归 XXX

  • Predict a quantitative YYY by single predictor variable XXX
    通过单个预测变量XXX 预测一个定量的YYY

Y≈β0+β1XY \approx \beta_0+\beta_1 XY≈β0​+β1​X

  • Example: sales≈β0+β1×TVsales \approx \beta_0+\beta_1\times TVsales≈β0​+β1​×TV.

  • β0\beta_0β0​, β1\beta_1β1​ are two unknown constants that represent the intercept and slope. [parameters, or coefficients.]
    β0\beta_0β0​ 和 β1\beta_1β1​ 是两个未知常数，分别表示截距和斜率。(参数或系数。)

Prediction

y^=β^0+β^1x.\hat y = \hat\beta_0+\hat\beta_1x.y^​=β^​0​+β^​1​x.

# How to estimate the coefficients 如何估计系数

Let y^i=β^0+β^1xi\hat{y}_i = \hat\beta_0+\hat\beta_1x_iy^​i​=β^​0​+β^​1​xi​ be the prediction for YYY based on the iiith value of XXX.
基于XXX 的第iii 个值，对YYY 的预测。

ei=yi−y^ie_i = y_i-\hat{y}_iei​=yi​−y^​i​ represents the iiith residual.
代表了第iii 个剩余。

Residual Sum of Squares RSS 剩余平方和

RSS=∑i=1nei2=∑i=1n(yi−y^i)2.\text{RSS} = \sum_{i=1}^n e_i^2 = \sum_{i=1}^n(y_i-\hat{y}_i)^2.RSS=i=1∑n​ei2​=i=1∑n​(yi​−y^​i​)2.

The least squares approach chooses β0\beta_0β0​ and β1\beta_1β1​ to minimize RSS.
最小二乘法选择 β0\beta_0β0​ 和 β1\beta_1β1​ 来最小化 RSS。

Fig 3.1. ISLR: For the Advertising data, the least squares fit for the regression of sales onto TV is shown.
对于 Advertising 数据，显示了 sales 与 TV 回归的最小二乘法。

The fit is found by minimizing the sum of squared errors.
拟合是通过最小化误差平方和来找到的。

Each grey line segment represents an error, and the fit makes a compro- mise by averaging their squares.
每个灰色线段代表一个误差，拟合通过平均它们的平方来做出妥协。

In this case a linear fit captures the essence of the relationship, although it is somewhat deficient in the left of the plot.
在这种情况下，线性拟合抓住了关系的本质，尽管它在图的左侧有些不足。

Fig. 3.2. ISLR: A simulated data set.
一个模拟数据集。

Left: The red line represents the true relationship, f(X)=2+3Xf(X) = 2 + 3Xf(X)=2+3X, which is known as the population regression line.
红线表示真实的关系，也就是总体回归线。

The blue line is the least squares line; it is the least squares estimate for f(X)f(X)f(X) based on the observed data, shown in black.
蓝线是最小二乘线；它是基于观测数据的 f(X)f(X)f(X) 的最小二乘估计，用黑色显示。

Right: The population regression line is again shown in red, and the least squares line in dark blue.
右图：总体回归线再次显示为红色，最小二乘线显示为深蓝色。

In light blue, ten least squares lines are shown, each computed on the basis of a separate random set of observations.
用浅蓝色表示十条最小平方线，每条线都是根据一组随机的观测数据计算出来的。

Each least squares line is different, but on average, the least squares lines are quite close to the population regression line.
每条最小二乘线不同，但平均而言，最小二乘线与总体回归线非常接近。

Here t=β^i−0SE(β^i)t= \frac{\hat \beta_i-0}{SE(\hat\beta_i)}t=SE(β^​i​)β^​i​−0​ is a t statistic. 是一个 t 统计

Question: Is there a relationship between the response YYY and predictor XXX?
响应变量YYY 和预测变量XXX 之间有关系吗？

We can do a hypothesis testing.
我们可以做一个假设检验。

  • check whether β1=0\beta_1=0β1​=0

    • Hypothesis test: H0:β1=0H_0:\beta_1=0H0​:β1​=0 vs. H1:β1≠0H_1: \beta_1\neq 0H1​:β1​=0.
    • a ttt-statistic measures the number of standard deviations that β1\beta_1β1​ is away from 0 (specifically, t=β^1−0SE(β^1)t= \frac{\hat \beta_1-0}{SE(\hat\beta_1)}t=SE(β^​1​)β^​1​−0​ with n−2n-2n−2 degrees of freedom)
      ttt - 统计测量 \BETA_1 远离 0 的标准偏差数（具体地说，t=β^1−0SE(β^1)t= \frac{\hat \beta_1-0}{SE(\hat\beta_1)}t=SE(β^​1​)β^​1​−0​，自由度为 n−2n-2n−2）

  • ppp-value

    • the probability of observing any value equal to t or larger; as usual! - the probability of seeing the data we saw under the H0H_0H0​
      观察到等于或大于 t 的任何值的概率；像往常一样！- 看到我们在H0H_0H0​ 下看到的数据的概率

    • in practice, we just read off the ttt-test. or read off the output of linear models.
      实际上，我们只是读出了ttt-test。或读出线性模型的输出。

问题：假设我们拒绝了无效假设，支持另一种假设，现在怎么办？

    自然的：量化模型适合数据的程度。

    线性回归拟合的质量通常使用两个相关量进行评估：
    • the residual standard error RSE and

# RSE

A measure of the lack of fit of the model simple linear regression model to the data:
一种衡量模型拟合不足的方法简单线性回归模型对数据的拟合:

RSE=1n−2RSS=1n−2∑i=1n(yi−y^i)2RSE = \sqrt{\frac{1}{n-2}RSS} = \sqrt{\frac{1}{n-2}\sum_{i=1}^n (y_i-\hat y_i)^2}RSE=n−21​RSS​=n−21​i=1∑n​(yi​−y^​i​)2​

  • If the predictions obtained using the model are very close to the true outcome values (y^i≈yi\hat y_i\approx y_iy^​i​≈yi​ for i = 1, ..., n), then RSE will be small
    如果使用模型得到的预测非常接近真实的结果值 (y^i≈yi\hat y_i\approx y_iy^​i​≈yi​ for i = 1, ..., n)，则 RSE 将很小

    • we can conclude that the model fits the data very well.
      我们可以得出结论，这个模型与数据吻合得很好。

  • If y^i\hat y_iy^​i​ is very far from yiy_iyi​ for one or more observations, then the RSE may be quite large
    如果 y^i\hat y_iy^​i​ 与 yiy_iyi​ 之间有很大的距离，那么 RSE 可能相当大

    • indicating that the model doesn’t fit the data well.
      这表明模型与数据的拟合并不好。

Interpretation 解释
The RSE provides an absolute measure of lack of fit.
RSE 提供了一种绝对的缺乏契合度的测量方法。

But since it is measured in the units of YYY , it is not always clear what constitutes a good RSE...
但由于它是以YYY 为单位来衡量的，所以并不总是清楚什么构成了一个好的 RSE……

# R2R^2R2

The R2R^2R2 statistic provides an alternative measure of fit (proportion):
R2R^2R2 统计提供了另一种拟合方法 (比例):

R2=TSS−RSSTSS=1−RSSTSSR^2 = \frac{TSS-RSS}{TSS}=1 - \frac{RSS}{TSS}R2=TSSTSS−RSS​=1−TSSRSS​

  • TSS = total sum of squares ∑i=1n(yi−yˉ)2\sum_{i=1}^n(y_i-\bar y)^2∑i=1n​(yi​−yˉ​)2
    where yˉ=1n∑i=1nyi\bar y = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^ny_iyˉ​=n1​∑i=1n​yi​
    总平方和
  • RSS = residual sum of squares ∑i=1n(yi−y^i)2\sum_{i=1}^n(y_i-\hat y_i)^2∑i=1n​(yi​−y^​i​)2
    残差平方和

R2R^2R2 measures the proportion of variability in YYY that can be explained using XXX
R2R^2R2 衡量的是可以用XXX 解释的YYY 的可变性比例

Interpretation 解释
Always between 0 and 1 (independent of scale of YYY).
总是在 0 和 1 之间 (与YYY 的比例无关)。

Question What's a good value?
问题是什么样的才算是良好的值
Can be challenging to determine ... in general, depends on the application.
很难确定… 一般来说，取决于应用程序。
