# The FFF Distribution FFF 分布

Suppose that we have a random sample of mmm observations from the normal population N(μ1,σ12)\mathcal{N}(\mu_1,\sigma_1^2)N(μ1​,σ12​) and an independent random sample of nnn observations from a second normal population N(μ2,σ22)\mathcal{N}(\mu_2,\sigma_2^2)N(μ2​,σ22​).
假设我们有一个随机样本 mmm 来自正态总体 N(μ1,σ12)\mathcal{N}(\mu_1,\sigma_1^2)N(μ1​,σ12​) ，以及一个独立的随机样本 nnn 来自第二个正态总体 N(μ2,σ22)\mathcal{N}(\mu_2,\sigma_2^2)N(μ2​,σ22​) 。

Then,

Fm−1,n−1=S12/σ12S22/σ22,F_{m-1,n-1} = \frac{S_1^2/\sigma_1^2}{S_2^2/\sigma_2^2},Fm−1,n−1​=S22​/σ22​S12​/σ12​​,

follows an FFF distribution with v1=m−1v_1 = m-1v1​=m−1 and v2=n−1v_2 = n-1v2​=n−1, where S1S_1S1​, S2S_2S2​ and σ1\sigma_1σ1​, σ2\sigma_2σ2​ are sample and population standard deviations of sample 1 and sample 2, respectively.
分别遵循自由度为 v1=m−1v_1 = m-1v1​=m−1 和 v2=n−1v_2 = n-1v2​=n−1 的 FFF 分布，其中 S1S_1S1​、S2S_2S2​ 和 σ1\sigma_1σ1​、σ2\sigma_2σ2​ 分别是样本 1 和样本 2 的样本标准差和总体标准差。

This FFF statistic can be used to compare variances from two independent groups.

# Conclusion

  1. Distribution of sample mean/difference between two means with known variance when sample size is large enough is normal distribution.
    当样本大小足够大时，两个已知方差的样本均值 / 差值分布是正态分布的。

  2. With unknown variance, the instance comes from the normal distribution, then the sample mean follows a ttt distribution
    方差未知，实例来自正态分布，则样本均值遵循 ttt 分布

  3. χ2\chi^2χ2 distribution is used for sample variance.
    χ2\chi^2χ2 分布用于样本方差。

  4. FFF distribution is used for the ratio of two variances.
    FFF 分布用于两个方差的比率。
